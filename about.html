My Story

How I got here

I started my journey with a Bachelor's in Computer Applications, where I discovered that the intersection of technology and human behaviour was where I wanted to spend my career. What began as a curiosity about how software feels to use quickly became a deep passion for designing experiences that actually work for people.

Over 4+ years I've worked across SaaS platforms, agri-tech e-commerce, and automation tooling — each role sharpening a different edge. From redesigning a logistics platform that reduced task creation time by 20%, to building scalable design systems that cut developer handoff friction by 25%, I've learned that good design lives in the details and scales through systems.

Today I'm focused on the places where AI meets design workflow — finding ways to move faster without sacrificing craft. I'm currently a Product Designer at Zopping Technology, and I'm always open to conversations about interesting problems worth solving.
