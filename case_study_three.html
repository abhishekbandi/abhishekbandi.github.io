Design for Indentation and Quotation Management

Research | UX Design | UI Design

In response to the growing need for a streamlined process for managing indents and quotations for customers, I have contributed to a user-friendly interface for sign-in, indent status tracking, and quotation management. The goal was to enhance the overall user experience and efficiency of the system.

Project Overview

In response to the increasing demand for streamlined trip management, our team has undertaken extensive research to develop a dedicated platform for our customers. This platform is designed to empower logistics parties to efficiently handle all customer trips through seamless integration with the indent creation process.

The user interface of the platform is user-friendly, for logistics customers. Customers can effortlessly create indents for their upcoming trips, and the logistics party promptly assigns quotations corresponding to these indents. Once the indent and quotation are mutually accepted, the trip officially commences. Customers gain real-time visibility into the status of their trips, allowing them to track progress until the trip completion.

Problem Statement

The early process of creating a trip was on collecting the customer trip details on communication channels like calls, messages, WhatsApp, and e-mail where an increase in the customers led to a manual process of creating the trip in an internal software.

Research and analysis (Empathy, Define, Ideate)
